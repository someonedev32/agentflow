About AgentFlow Pro

AgentFlow Pro is an offline-first iOS app built specifically for real estate agents who need speed, reliability, and simplicity while working with clients.

Our Mission

Our mission is simple: help real estate agents respond faster, stay organized, and look professional — even without an internet connection.

Why AgentFlow Pro?

Real estate agents spend most of their time on the move: in cars, at property visits, on phone calls, and in areas with poor connectivity. AgentFlow Pro keeps all listings stored locally on your iPhone, so you can search, update, and share properties instantly.

What We Focus On

  • Offline-first performance
  • Fast property search and filtering
  • Instant WhatsApp sharing
  • Multi-language support
  • Clean, agent-focused design

Who It’s For

AgentFlow Pro is designed for independent agents and small agencies who want a reliable personal workspace for their listings — not a public marketplace.
